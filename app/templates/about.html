How Our Algorithm Works

We use an optimized Naive Bayes classifier for crop recommendation, ideal for agricultural data.

  • Training: Learns from thousands of real crop samples.
  • Prediction: Calculates the probability of each crop for your parameters.
  • Selection: Recommends the crop with the highest probability and confidence.
